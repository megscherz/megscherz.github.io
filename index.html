How I Work

I believe quality assurance isn't just about catching bugs—it's about building confidence in software. I aim to catch potential failures early by designing scalable, maintainable test automation frameworks and collaborating closely with developers and stakeholders. My approach is proactive and detail-oriented, with an emphasis on testability and early feedback loops.

I'm comfortable owning test pipelines in CI/CD environments and troubleshooting flaky tests to restore pipeline health quickly. I see QA as a partner in delivering better user experiences—not a gatekeeper, but a guide toward cleaner, more reliable code.
